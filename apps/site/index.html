Features

Built for the bench, not the boardroom.

Every feature is designed around the molecular cloning workflow researchers actually use — from plasmid management to multi-step assembly planning.

GenBank import

Import plasmids from GenBank or SnapGene files. Features, annotations, and topology are preserved and visualized instantly.

Digest simulation

Pick restriction enzymes and simulate digests in silico. See exactly where cuts land, what fragments you get, and which overhangs result.

Ligation verification

Check overhang compatibility before you ligate. Geney verifies that your fragments assemble correctly and shows the resulting construct.

Procedure builder

Chain digests and ligations into multi-step procedures with a visual node editor. Validate the entire pipeline before running it.

Local-first

Everything runs on your machine. Your sequences never leave your disk. No accounts, no cloud, no data sharing.

Content-addressed storage

Sequences, features, and constructs are identified by their content. Import the same plasmid twice and it deduplicates automatically.

How it works

From plasmid to procedure in minutes.

Import your constructs, design your cloning strategy, and validate it computationally before heading to the bench.

Build your library

Import plasmids from GenBank files or browse the Addgene catalog. Organize constructs into workspaces with drag-and-drop folders.

Design your procedure

Add digest and ligation steps to a visual workflow. Select enzymes, wire fragments between operations, and see outputs update in real time.

Validate & export

Run the full procedure to verify every step produces valid products. Inspect resulting constructs with an interactive sequence viewer, then export as GenBank.
